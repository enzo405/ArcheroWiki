Glyphs:(reddit guide)
There is 2 type of glyphs :
‚Ä¢ Base Glyphs (without rank) --->
‚Ä¢ Special Glyphs (rank A / S) --->
How to get glyphs ??
  • you need to sacrifice stuff starting from epic to ancient legendary
  • When you sacrificed your stuff you can get random rarity & number of the same glyphs
  • There is a chance you will get duplicates of the same Glyphs.
  • Each item gives different glyphs
Rarities: Rare is the lowest rarity, Glyphs can be upgraded all the way up to Mythic.
(Rare ‚Üí Epic ‚ÜíPerfect Epic ‚Üí Legendary ‚Üí Ancient Legendary ‚Üí Mythic)
Be careful, a legendary stuff can give you up to 1 rare glyphs (wich is really bad)
Refine:(reddit guide)
There are 4 Glyph slots.
How to unlock them:
    Refinement rarity starts at Epic, to unlock the second slot, Perfect Epic, you need to refine 8 times. Same goes for the next rarities.
  • 1st Slot (Epic) => Can only equip Normal Glyphs
  • 2nd Slot (Perfect Epic) => Unlocks Special Glyph slot
  • 3rd Slot (Legendary) => Unlocks Normal Glyph slot
  • 4th Slot (Ancient Legendary) => Unlocks Normal Glyph slot

Upgrading Star levels:
  • Epic 1 --> Epic 2 = 1 Epic Item + +
    7 to Perfect Epic => 2 Epics
  • Perfect Epic 1 --> Perfect Epic 2 = 1 Epic Item + +
    7 to Legendary => 2 Perfect Epic
  • Legendary 1 --> Legendary 2 = 1 Perfect Epic Item + +
    7 to ALE => 2 Legendary
  • Ancient Legendary 1 --> Ancient Legendary 2 = 1 Legendary Item + +
    7 to Mythic => 2 Legendary
  • Mythic 1 --> 2 = 2 Legendary Items + +
